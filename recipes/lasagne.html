Lasagne

Description

This dish is delicious, tasty, and one of my personal favourites.
It doesn't take long to make and is great for any occasion.

Ingrediants

  • beef mince
  • tomatoes
  • pasta sheets

Steps

  1. Boil the water for your pasta sheets.
  2. Whilst the water is boiling, prepare ingrediants. Chop tomatoes and onions, halve the papper
  3. Heat a large frying pan with a small amount of oil on a medium/high heat. Brown the mince
