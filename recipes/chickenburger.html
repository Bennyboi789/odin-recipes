Chicken burger

Description

This amazing dish is incredibly easy to

make and very tasty too. All you need

for this amazing dish is just a couple of

ingrediantsand is highly customisable for

those picky eaters.

Ingrediants

  • chicken
  • brioche
  • favourite salad
  • favourite dressing/s

Steps

  1. Heat a medium frying pay with a small amount of oil. Fry chicken for 10mins or untill there is no pink in the middle
  2. Turn the chicken occasionally. Whilst the chicken is frying, prepare the chips and salad.
  3. Once the chicken has no pink in the middle, its time to shred it using two forks
